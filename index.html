By DMTesseracT Ltd

The Vision

War of Being is an ambitious First Person, RPG title pushing the boundaries of Unreal Engine 5.

In our pursuit of a Rock Solid foundation, we’ve spent countless hours optimizing our core systems to provide great gameplay as well as smoother implementation for our designers.

We wished we had access to plugins like these when we started, and recognise how useful many of them can be across a plethora of different projects. On top of this, we believe that a rising tide lifts all boats. So instead of keeping these systems behind closed doors, we are releasing our decoupled source code to the community. By sharing our "sealed" production classes, we hope to help other developers bypass the foundational hurdles and get straight to creating great experiences. We don't expect anything from you for using these systems! But we'd love to see what crazy things people come up with. We're also open to any and all feedback, so feel free to hit up our lead dev Adam over at adam@dmtesseract.com!

Plugins

License information

We believe in the power of shared tools. Our plugins are released under a custom Source-Available License. This means they are 100% free for you to use in your games (commercial or otherwise), but they are protected from being repackaged and resold. We want to support developers, not resellers.

Expectations

Please keep in mind that these plugins are actively in development and are constantly updated while we build the War of Being game. As such, designer facing UI can often be a little unfriendly vs Fab store products! These will be ironed out over the course of development, but the key focus of our time is functionality for a shipping build.
